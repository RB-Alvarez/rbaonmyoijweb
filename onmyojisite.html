Somewhat Boiled Down, No Frills Guide to Onmyoji

What is Onmyoji?


(Steam description) Onmyoji is a turn-based fantasy strategy game with PVP or PVE battles, where you can strengthen your beloved shikigami to build your

dream tactical team and defeat various demons to become the ultimate onmyoji. Cross-platform play available for both Steam and English mobile versions.

Units

(referred to as "shikigami" in-game, but are called "units" here for the sake of simplicity)

Summoning!

Your primary source of obtaining new units. Unfortunately it is RNG/luck based. The summoning chance correlates with the rarity of a unit, which goes from N, R, SR, SSR/SP, highest to lowest.

Roles!

Each unit you summon plays a specific role. Put simply, they are either a damage dealer or support. Although, those two categories can be split down even further into the following.

ST DPS: Damage dealers that have strong Single Target damage skills

AoE DPS: Damage dealers that have strong Area Of Effect skills (i.e. can hit more than one target)

Sustain: Helps the team stay alive through healing after damage is taken, or shields to prevent damage beforehand

Accelerator: Ensures the team can act before the enemy (more detail in the "Battle System" segment)

Orb Generator: Generates additional resources for continuous special skill usage

Stats!

Each unit has a statline composed of the following and given a letter rating. DPS will have their statline leaning more towards ATTACK compared to support units whose lines lean towards defensive stats.

This is one of the many things that set each unit apart from others in the same role.

ATTACK: Affects how much damage a unit will deal

HP: The amount of health the unit has, once this number reaches 0, they die (can also be interpreted as the numerical value for how much damage a unit can take)

DEFENSE: Affects how much damage the unit will take (LESS defense = MORE damage taken)

SPEED: Affects who acts first in a battle

Crit: The % chance of dealing bonus damage

Crit DMG: What % of the original attack gets added when dealing a Crit. If x = orignial amount of damage, x + CritDMG(x) = Total bonus damage

Effect Hit: The additional % chance of an extra effect taking place if applicanble. So, if an attack has a 10% base chance of stunning the target, the game takes base chance + Effect Hit = actual % chance of stunning

Effect RES: The % chance of RESISTING additional effects. Pretty much the same way DEF is for ATK

Skills


1. Name

2. Cost: How many orbs they cost (more info on orbs under 'Battle System'). This line may say "Passive" which is a persisitent effect that could have conditions for activating.

3. Quote: A line said by the unit in relation to the skill

4. Skill Level: The higher this level, the more powerful the skill is (increased with black darumas or duplicate units)

5. Description: Details on what the skill does

6. Definitions: Defines terms in the description highlighted in blue

7. Level Up Effects: Details on how each level up affects the skill

The Process


1. Select which units you'll bring into the battle

2. The unit with the highest speed goes first and the next fastest follow.

3. Each unit can perform one skill each turn. Units usually have 3 skills or 2 skills and a passive.

4. Select your target(s)

5. An animation will play showing the skill.

6. Repeat until either side loses all their units

The UI


1. Boss HP bar (if applicable)

2. Enemy Units

3. Your Units

4. Movement bar: displays who will move next. Ally = Yellow, Enemy = Red

5. Health bar. Blue Icons = buffs, Red Icons = debuffs

6. Current Unit's skills

7. Current Unit's Soul set

8. Orb Bar: Each ally turn increases the blue segments at the bottom by 1. When the bottom bar fills, you gain +3/4/5 orbs (blue flames).

The amount increases each turn to a maximum of 5.

9. Auto/Manual and Battle Speed options
